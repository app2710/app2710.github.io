Research Interests

Chemical engineering focus areas

Carbon Capture & Storage

Solid sorbent development, TSA/VSA swing processes, and techno-economic analysis for NGCC and industrial point sources.

Heterogeneous Catalysis

Catalyst characterization, kinetic modeling, high-throughput screening, and structure–activity relationships in porous materials.

Reactor Engineering

Fixed-bed design, mass transfer analysis, Thiele modulus, scale-up from lab to pilot, and process intensification.

Adsorption Science

Isotherm modeling, kinetic & diffusion analysis, breakthrough curve characterization, and material shaping for industrial deployment.

Zeolite & Geopolymer Chemistry

In-situ crystallization, synthesis optimization via DOE, and composite material design for selective gas separation.

Techno-Economic Analysis

Process modeling, cost of capture estimation, sensitivity analysis, and feasibility assessment for emerging CCUS technologies.

Hobbies

Outside the lab

📷

Landscape & Astrophotography

I shoot with a Nikon Z5 full-frame mirrorless camera, primarily targeting landscape and deep-sky objects. For astrophotography, I use a Sky-Watcher Star Adventurer 2i tracking mount to capture faint nebulae and galaxies.

Recent targets include M81/M82 and the Heart & Soul Nebulae from dark-sky sites south of Calgary. I process images in Siril and post to Instagram as @app2710_photography.

🔭

Stargazing & Astro Events

Regular dark-sky trips to sites in Vulcan County and the foothills west of Calgary for visual observing and imaging sessions.

  • Polar alignment and PA drift correction with the SA 2i
  • Planning sessions around lunar cycles and Bortle class maps
  • Tracking meteor showers, conjunctions, and occultations
🥾

Hiking
